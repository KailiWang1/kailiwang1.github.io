Brief Biography

Dr. Kaili Wang received her Ph.D degree from Min Li's Group at School of Computer Science and Engineering in Central South University in 2023. She is currently a lecture at School of Computer Science and Technology in Donghua University. The main focuses are AI-based Bioinformatics, Structural Bioinformatics, Deep Learning, Large Language Model.
